Tips on how to reduce Carbon Footprint


Work on Recycling your plastic and your cardboard!


Work on reducing the pollution that goes in the air to protect our nature and animals!


A good way to reduce carbon footprint is by simply using less electricity


Limit the amount of energy you use from going place to place.


Another good way to reduce it is also reuse your refill water bottles, reuse grocery bags,

and see how long you can go without creating any trash problems.


Doing two loads of laundry weekly in cold water instead of hot or warm water can save up to 500

pounds of carbon dioxide each year.


Balcony gardens are a great solution for urbanites looking to up their curb appeal while lowering their enviromental impact.


Doing all your errands in one day will help you cut back on unnecessary trips throughout the week. After

all, the less you drive, the more you reduce your carbon footprint!


Buying locally grown produce whenever possible drastically reduces the emissions caused by freight

shipping. Plus, it’s a great way to support your community’s economy!
