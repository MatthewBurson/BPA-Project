How Carbon Footprint is effecting our planet?

Large amounts of Carbon Footprint and the increasing amount of it is effecting our planet. Carbon Footprint is causing Climate change which has been causing problems like the glaciers melting which is rising the ocean level and is flooding many places near the coast. Carbon footprint is also cousing urban air pollution wich leads to toxic acid rain.
